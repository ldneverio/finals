Travel Destinations

North Philippines

Batad Rice Terraces

see an endless sea of green before you, and mountain slopes covered in rice terraces stretching away towards the horizon.

Vigan

home to many wonderfully-preserved colonial-era buildings and cobbled streets

Banaue Rice Terraces

the most scenic setting imaginable, the small town of Banaue is primarily visited for the majestic views of the rice terrace-covered mountains and valleys surrounding it

Hundred Islands National Park

make the most of the stunning scenery, it is well worth overnighting on an island and spending a few days swimming and snorkeling in the national parks turquoise waters

Pagudpud

boasts breathtakingly beautiful beaches lined with gorgeous, gently swaying palm trees and glittering turquoise waters

Sagada

Set in a beautiful location amongst the mountains, Sagada is an outdoor adventurers dream

Mount Pulag National Park

The highest peak in the whole of Luzon, Mount Pulag towers imperiously over its surroundings. It’s easy to see why various indigenous people consider it to be sacred

San Juan

With some of the best breaks in the country, San Juan (La Union) is a surfer’s dream, with beginners and experts alike heading to the laidback town to try out its epic waves and swells
