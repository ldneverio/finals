Check the Routes and Schedule

Main routes focuses on the buses going to the North, which includes the buses Northbound within NCR and buses going to provinces north of the NCR. This app includes features that show the buses’ arrival and departure times according to the bus liner’s policies, the route used by the buses bound to a specific location, and the availability of seats within each buses scheduled to depart at a given time. As such, this application is expected to be more of a bulletin board than an actual travel guide. Sales of tickets and lodging are also included on this application.
